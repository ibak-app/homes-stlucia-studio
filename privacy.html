Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

homes.stlucia.studio ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by stlucia.studio and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account details: name, email address, phone number, and password when you register an account.
  • Agent profiles: agency name, real estate licence number, profile photo, biography, and contact preferences.
  • Property listings: property address, description, photographs, price (in EC$ and US$), listing type (sale or rent), and CBI eligibility status.
  • Enquiry messages: messages you send to agents or property owners through our contact forms.
  • Payment information: billing name and email address. Card numbers are handled directly by Stripe and are never stored on our servers.
  • Saved searches and favourites: properties and search filters you save to your account.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: pages viewed, search queries entered, filters applied, and listing details clicked.
  • Device information: browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and referring URL.
  • IP address: used to estimate your approximate location (country or region) for currency display purposes. We do not store full IP addresses.
  • Cookies and local storage: session tokens, preference settings, and saved search state. See Section 5 for full details.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • If you sign in using Google or Facebook OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from that provider.
  • If you are a registered user of talent.stlucia.studio or another stlucia.studio service, your basic profile (name and email) is accessible within the shared stlucia.studio account system.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Saint Lucia Data Protection Act:

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to provide the service you have requested — displaying listings, processing agent subscriptions, handling property enquiries, and managing your account.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Where you have given explicit consent — marketing emails, property alert notifications, optional analytics cookies (GA4, Facebook Pixel), and newsletter subscriptions. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Where we have a legitimate business interest that does not override your rights — fraud prevention, platform security, aggregated analytics for service improvement, and abuse detection.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where processing is necessary to comply with applicable law — maintaining payment records for tax purposes (7 years per Saint Lucia tax regulations), responding to lawful authority requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: display property listings, connect buyers and renters with agents, and process payments for subscriptions and featured listings.
  • Account management: authenticate your identity and send transactional emails including password reset, listing status updates, and enquiry notifications.
  • Personalisation: remember saved searches, recently viewed properties, and display prices in your preferred currency (EC$ or US$).
  • Platform improvement: analyse aggregated usage patterns to improve search relevance, page performance, and feature priorities. We never use individual behaviour data for this purpose without anonymisation.
  • Marketing communications: send you new listing alerts, property market reports, and platform news — only if you have opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in each message or in your account settings.
  • Legal compliance: maintain records required under Saint Lucia's Data Protection Act and respond to lawful requests from competent authorities.
  • Fraud prevention: detect and block abuse, spam listings, and unauthorised account access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only in the following circumstances:

  • Supabase (database and authentication): Our backend is hosted on Supabase (US East region). Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database with row-level security enforced — only you and authorised parties can access your records. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • Stripe (payments): Subscription and listing payment processing. Stripe operates under PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance. We share your name and email; we never share card details.
  • Plausible Analytics: Privacy-first, cookie-free analytics hosted in the EU. No personal identifiers are shared — aggregate page-view data only.
  • Google Analytics 4: Anonymised usage analytics. IP addresses are truncated before storage. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser extension.
  • Cloudflare: CDN and DDoS protection. Cloudflare processes network traffic on our behalf but does not retain personal data beyond transient request handling.
  • Agents: When you submit an enquiry for a property, your name, email address, and message are forwarded to the listing agent. Agents are bound by our Terms of Service and may not use your contact details for unsolicited communications unrelated to your enquiry.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose personal data if required by a court order, subpoena, or applicable law in Saint Lucia or another jurisdiction with lawful authority over us.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to this same policy.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: your data is retained for as long as your account remains active.
  • Closed accounts: we delete personal profile data within 90 days of account closure, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Property listings: removed or expired listings are archived for 12 months for dispute resolution purposes, then permanently deleted.
  • Enquiry messages: retained for 24 months to assist with any disputes, then deleted.
  • Analytics event logs: aggregate event data is retained for 24 months.
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required by accounting and tax regulations in Saint Lucia.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use a minimal set of cookies and browser storage mechanisms:

  • Session cookie (essential): Issued by Supabase Auth to keep you logged in. Required for core functionality. Expires when you close your browser or after 7 days of inactivity, whichever comes first.
  • Preference storage (functional): Stored in browser localStorage. Saves currency preference (EC$ or US$), search filters, and recently viewed listings. Has no expiry date and is cleared when you clear your browser data.
  • Analytics cookies (optional): Plausible Analytics is entirely cookie-free. Google Analytics 4 may set first-party cookies for session measurement. You may decline analytics cookies via our consent banner at first visit. Declining does not affect core functionality.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you in a readable format.
  • Correction: update inaccurate or incomplete information via your account settings or by contacting us directly.
  • Deletion: request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Certain data may be retained to comply with legal obligations or for legitimate dispute resolution purposes.
  • Portability: request your profile and listing data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).
  • Objection to marketing: opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in each message or via your account notification settings.
  • Withdrawal of consent: where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.
  • Restriction: request that we restrict processing of your data in specific circumstances, such as while a correction request is being assessed.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@stlucia.studio. We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and respond in full within 30 days.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for all data in transit, row-level security policies in our database (ensuring users can only access their own data), salted and hashed passwords, and regular security reviews. No internet-based system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorised access to your account, please change your password immediately and contact us at privacy@stlucia.studio.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by GDPR Article 33.
  • Notify affected individuals without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms, as required by GDPR Article 34.
  • Document the breach including its nature, categories and approximate number of individuals affected, likely consequences, and measures taken to address it.
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach, including revoking compromised credentials and notifying affected third parties (e.g., agents whose data may have been exposed).

To report a suspected breach, contact us immediately at privacy@stlucia.studio.

10. Children

homes.stlucia.studio is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

11. International Transfers

Our platform is based in Saint Lucia. Data is stored in Supabase's US East (North Virginia) data centre. By using our service, you acknowledge that your data may be processed outside your country of residence. We rely on Supabase's standard contractual protections for any such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify registered users by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
